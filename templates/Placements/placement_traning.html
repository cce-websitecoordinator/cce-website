Placement Training

The Placement & Training Cell strongly believes that the key to quality placements is in providing quality training to students. The requirements of the industry is identified through interaction with industry officials, campus recruitment feedback, employer feedback and Alumni feedback. Training activities are planned accordingly which is a mix of offline and online training. Sessions to improve communication skills, remove stage fear, build team work, improve leadership qualities are also included in the training programs.
