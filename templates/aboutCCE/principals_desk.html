Message

The idea of Christ College of Engineering was born out of the aspirations of locals from different strata of society who felt the need for a world-class institution offering professional education in the mini education hub called Irinjalakuda. Today Christ College of Engineering is well on its way to becoming a much sought-after destination for engineering education due to the efforts of all stakeholders. Our students are at the center of this success story.

We leave no stone unturned in ensuring that along with academics, our students get the right amount of exposure required for tomorrow’s successful global citizen, through the various programs held at our campus. Successful engineering careers don’t happen by chance. Many factors contribute to this success. We at Christ College of Engineering are committed to providing our students with the right platform to launch themselves into a successful career. Experiential learning, developing one’s portfolio, and building one’s network are fundamental to achieving this.

We believe that with the kind of transformative learning model in place here, we are on our way to making a positive impact on our society and nation at large. Do you want your success story to take shape here? Well then, come to our launchpad. Come join Christ College of Engineering.
