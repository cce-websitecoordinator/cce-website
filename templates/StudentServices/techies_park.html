About

As part of building a strong startup ecosystem in Kerala, one of Talrop's goals is to bridge the industry-academic gap within 5 years. For this, Talrop has introduced the '10000 Jobs Mission' at selected colleges across Kerala as the first phase. This mission is coordinated by Talrop's placement platform Jobified, students are moulded into industry-ready professionals by Talrop's edtech platform Steyp and are placed through Talrop's IT services job platform Tegain. Initially, students from the first year of college were given orientation about the program and interested students were interviewed. 15 students who are shortlisted and joined from the college are undergoing training under expert engineers. The students who complete this training in their first year of college are given part-time opportunities in their second year of college and once graduated, they are placed at Talrop and associated companies. Talrop's 10000 Jobs Mission project is handled in Christ College of Engineering, Irinjalakuda by Ms. Anmary Jiju who is the Co-founder of Jobified and the Business Development Head at Talrop.

About

  1. Techies Park

    Techies Park is an infrastructure project by Talrop, built as a part of Talrop's mission of building a strong startup ecosystem in Kerala.

  2. T-Fut (Team Future)

    A campus community program powered by Talrop aims at creating campus life more exciting by reducing the industry-academics gap and providing exciting opportunities directly from Talrop.

  3. Talrop's Jobs Mission

    As part of building a strong startup ecosystem in Kerala, one of Talrop's goals is to bridge the industry-academic gap within 5 years. For this, Talrop has introduced the '10000 Jobs Mission' at selected colleges across Kerala as the first phase.

  4. Talrop's Startup School

    One of the major project Talrop undertakens as part of building a strong startup ecosystem in Kerala is Startup School. The project is currently implementing in 100 selected campuses across Kerala, which started with Christ College of Engineering, Irinjalakuda.

  5. Full Stack Development Training

    Tegain, an initiative of Talrop provides full stack training program and web development assistance to students to upgrade their technical skills.
