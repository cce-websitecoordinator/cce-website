About

The Parent Teacher Association is a statutorily constituted elected body which aims at the overall development of the college. Parental co-operation in creating a healthy campus atmosphere is of utmost importance. The PTA aspires for co-operation and participation of parents in full measure, in the wholesome activities of the college. The PTA of Christ College of Engineering, Irinjalakuda aims to foster and promote good relationship among members of the faculty, students and guardians of students.
