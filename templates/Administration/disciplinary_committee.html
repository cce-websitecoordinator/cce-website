Disciplinary Committee

The Disciplinary Committee is a vital component of our campus community, dedicated to fostering a calm and peaceful academic atmosphere. Our primary objectives include developing and refining norms to regulate student behavior, reducing conflicts and violence, and encouraging positive practices. The committee actively maintains strict discipline within the campus, monitors students' punctuality, handles complaints impartially, and administers appropriate punishments when rules are violated. By upholding these functions, we aim to create a conducive environment for learning and personal development, ensuring the well-being and success of our students.
